Dixa's Q2 2026 update highlights the launch of promise detection for its Mim AI agent, which flags commitments the agent cannot fulfill to prevent hallucination. The roadmap shifts from measuring customer service by efficiency and satisfaction toward a new Value Experience framework that connects support conversations to revenue events. Q3 priorities include voice capabilities for Mim, automated knowledge article generation, and sales attribution tracking.
