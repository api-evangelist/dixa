This guide explains the EU's new right of withdrawal requirements under Directive (EU) 2023/2673, effective June 19, 2026, which require online merchants to provide a clearly labeled withdrawal button letting consumers cancel purchases within 14 days without penalty. It details compliance requirements, banned practices like retention pop-ups, potential penalties up to 4% of annual turnover, and how support channels must properly handle withdrawal requests.
